<ParamField body="patientIds" type="array" required="No*">
  Array of patient IDs to assign to the cohort. Mutually
  exclusive with <code>all</code>.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="all" type="boolean" required="No*">
  Flag to assign all patients to the cohort. Mutually
  exclusive with <code>patientIds</code>.
</ParamField>

\*Either <code>patientIds</code> or <code>all</code> must be provided, but not both.
